An excellent client brief, feedback on our proposals and great communication with the architects and builder were important factors in the success of this project which was designed and managed for a client whilst overseas. The Binova kitchen in oak veneer is functional and elegant. Two integrated fridges and a pivot door to a small pantry allow everything to be put away out of sight when not in use. A practical concept in an open plan kitchen.
